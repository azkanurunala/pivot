One swipe. Infinite depth. Master the angle.

Need help?

  • A level feels impossible? Every level has a solvable angle. Miss five times and the in-game Show the path / Show the angle hints unlock.
  • Restoring a purchase? Open the Pivot Pro sheet and tap Restore purchase, or redeem a gift code under Settings → Redeem.
  • Lost progress? Progress is stored on-device; reinstalling or resetting clears it. Pro is restored via your Apple ID.
